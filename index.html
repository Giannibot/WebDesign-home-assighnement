    Games:

    Roulette:


    A ball is spun on a wheel with numbered slots and

    players bet on the outcome. The winning bet is

    determined by where the ball lands on the wheel.

    Blackjack:


    Get a hand value as close to 21 as possible without

    goring over. The player with the highest hand value or

    closest to 21 without going over wins the game.

    Baccarat:


    A card game which players try to get a hand value as

    close to 9 as possible. The player with the highest

    hand value or closest to 9 wins the game.

    Craps:


    A dice game played where players bet on the

    outcome of a pair of dice being rolled by a "shooter."

    The shooter must roll a certain combination of

    numbers to win.

    Poker:


    A card game where players bet on their hand and

    try to win the pot. Players win by having the

    highest-ranking hand.

    Sic Bo:


    Dice game based on chance. All you need to do

    is place your chips on a bet box on the table.

    The dice are shaken and if the outcome of the

    roll matches your bet, you win.

    Keno:


    Played with tickets bearing numbers in squares,

    usually from 1 to 80. A player marks as many of these

    numbers as up to the permitted maximum, after which

    registers the ticket and pays according to how many

    numbers are marked.

    Slot machine:


    operated by inserting coins into a slot and pulling a

    handle that activates a set of spinning symbols on

    wheels, the final alignment of which determines

    the payoff that is released into a receptacle at the

    bottom.

    Bingo:


    A game of probability in which players mark off

    numbers on cards as the numbers are drawn

    randomly by a caller, the winner being the first

    person to mark off all their numbers.
